Home

Hello and welcome to this less known part of the Internet!

So, why does this site exist? It was created as a part of a course project. The task was to create a website using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. This is what I came up with. The original idea was to make this an art site with some information about myself. It didn't suite the course tasks quite so well in the end so I improvised a lot and tried to make it look like there's some sense in there.
It may look messy, but enjoy your stay nevertheless!

About Me

Who am I really? You might want to know something about me since I made this site and you're here. If not, reading this isn't obligatory.
I am a 20-year-old student at Kajaani University of Applied Sciences. I'm studying to become a BBA in information technology and my specialty is game graphics. I mostly draw animal characters and dragons because me and my friends have quite a few of them and I simply love dragons.
I enjoy designing characters and drawing them and writing stuff. I have about ten years of experience in character design. I spend my freetime watching TV series and movies and reading books and sometimes fanfiction. I also roleplay online and play console games. Of course I also draw whenever I have time and inspiration.
My favorite game series are probably Assassin's Creed and Uncharted and single games inFAMOUS: Second Son, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt and Beyond: Two Souls.
My taste in music is quite wide. I enjoy soundtracks, old and new rock and metal as well as all kinds of random stuff outside of those main categories.
